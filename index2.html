Solar System & Eclipse Mini-Project

About the Solar System

The solar system consists of the Sun and the celestial objects that are bound to it by gravity, including the eight planets and their moons, dwarf planets, asteroids, comets, and more.

Eclipses

An eclipse occurs when one celestial body moves into the shadow of another. There are two main types of eclipses: solar and lunar.
